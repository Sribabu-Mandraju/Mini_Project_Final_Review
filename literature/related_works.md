## Related Works

| Research Work | Approach | Limitation |
|---|---|---|
| **Blockchain Donation Systems (2022)** | Uses blockchain to record and track donations | No automatic fund distribution to victims |
| **Helpchain Framework (2023)** | Registers and tracks victims using blockchain | Does not support fund disbursement |
| **Smart Contract Charity Systems (2021)** | Uses smart contracts for managing donations | Lacks proper victim verification |
| **Blockchain Disaster Management (2024)** | Uses blockchain for coordination and data sharing | Complex system with limited direct aid delivery |
| **Sharding-based Aid Systems (2024)** | Improves scalability of blockchain-based aid | Missing secure identity verification |
